Notice:

  • PaCO2 is measured in the unit of mm Hg
  • HCO3- is measured in the unit of mEq/L
  • Reference ranges: pH 7.36 - 7.44, PaCO2 36 – 44 mm Hg, HCO3- 22 – 26 mEq/L
  • Primary respiratory acid-base disorder has acute and chronic forms
  • Response analyzer is used to evaulate if a secondary acid-base disorder coexists (primary disorders only)
