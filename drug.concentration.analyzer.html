Notice:

  • S is the salt factor (proportion)
  • F is the bioavailability factor (proportion)
  • tau is the dose interval (hr)
  • Tinf is the duration of infusing (min)
  • Vd is volume of distribution (L)
  • k is the elimination rate constant (hr-1)
  • ninteger is the number of dose(s) administered
  • Cn is the drug concentration after n dose(s), in unit of mcg/mL
  • Css is the steady state drug concentration, in unit of mcg/mL
  • C is the total drug concentration after loading dose administered, in unit of mcg/mL
  • CLD is the drug concentration contributed via that loading dose, in unit of mcg/mL
