Notice:

  • PaO2 is the oxygen pressure in arterial blood, in unit of mm Hg
  • PvO2 is the oxygen pressure in venous blood, in unit of mm Hg
  • SaO2 is the oxyhemoglobin saturation of aterial blood
  • SvO2 is the oxyhemoglobin saturation of venous blood
  • Hgb is the blood concentration of hemoglobin, in unit of g/dL
  • Cardiac output is the unit of L/min
  • Bronchial circulation proportion refers to the percentage of cardiac output toward bronnchial circulation
  • Core temperature is related to oxygen physical dissolving coefficent
  • O2 delivery is the oxygen capacity carried by arterial blood
  • O2 uptake is the oxygen amount extracted by tissue
  • Extraction ratio
    is the ratio between O2 uptake and O2 delivery, which has a limit of approxmiately 0.5
  • O2 reservation is oxygen capacity carried by venous blood
